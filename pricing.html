Frequently Asked Questions

Are there any transaction fees?

No. Zero transaction fees on any plan. You keep 100% of your sales.

Can I upgrade or downgrade anytime?

Yes. Change your plan at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately. Downgrades take effect at the end of your billing period.

What counts as a "card" toward my limit?

Each unique line item in your inventory counts as one card, regardless of quantity. If you have 10 copies of the same card, that's 1 card toward your limit.

What card types are supported?

All types: Pokemon, Magic: The Gathering, Yu-Gi-Oh!, sports cards, Lorcana, One Piece, Digimon, and more. If it's a card, we support it.

Do I need a credit card for the free plan?

No. Sign up and start using Card Sync for free. No credit card required.
