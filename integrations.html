Google Sheets Not Connected

Sync your Card Sync inventory with a Google Sheet. Any changes you make in the sheet will update your inventory, and vice versa. Great for managing inventory on the go or sharing with your team.

No sheet connected

How to Connect

1

Create a Google Sheet

Go to sheets.google.com and create a new blank spreadsheet. You can name it anything you like (e.g. "My Card Inventory").

2

Share it with Card Sync

Click the Share button in your Google Sheet, then add this email address as an Editor:

card-sync-sheets@card-sync-491400.iam.gserviceaccount.com

Make sure to select "Editor" (not Viewer) so Card Sync can write to your sheet.

3

Paste your Sheet URL below

Copy the full URL from your browser's address bar and paste it into the connection form below. It looks like:
https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/abc123.../edit

4

Connect and sync

Click Connect Sheet and Card Sync will verify access. Then use the sync buttons to push your inventory to the sheet or pull changes back. Your sheet headers will be set up automatically.

How to Connect

1

Click "Connect eBay"

You'll be redirected to eBay's login page to authorize Card Sync to manage your listings.

2

Log in and approve

Sign into your eBay seller account and click "Agree" to grant Card Sync access to your inventory and listings.

3

Start syncing

Once connected, use the sync buttons to push your inventory to eBay as live listings. Card Sync handles the rest.
